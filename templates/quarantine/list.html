Spam Filter Status

SpamAssassin is active and scanning all inbound/outbound emails via milter.

How it works

  • Every email passes through SpamAssassin before delivery
  • SpamAssassin scores emails based on rules, Bayesian filtering, and network tests
  • Emails with high spam scores are flagged in headers (X-Spam-Score)
  • Check email headers in your mail client to see spam scores

Spam Score Headers

Look for these headers in received emails:

HeaderDescription
X-Spam-ScoreSpam score (higher = more spammy)
X-Spam-StatusYes/No with score breakdown
X-Spam-FlagYES if flagged as spam

Typical Score Thresholds

ScoreAction
< 3Clean — delivered normally
3 - 5Suspicious — flagged in headers
5 - 10Likely spam — flagged prominently
> 10Almost certainly spam
